Cookie Policy

This policy explains how Simplify My Life and our partners use cookies and similar technologies across our sites and applications.

Last updated: June 10, 2024

What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device. They help applications remember your preferences and provide essential functionality. We also use local storage and similar technologies for the same purpose.

How we use cookies

  • Maintain session state across our static and interactive tools.
  • Remember preferences such as theme or difficulty settings.
  • Measure basic, anonymous analytics to understand usage patterns and improve the experience.

Third-party cookies

Embedded services like Buy Me a Coffee or analytics providers may set cookies when you interact with their widgets. These cookies are controlled by the respective provider and subject to their policies.

Managing cookies

Most browsers allow you to clear or block cookies and local storage through their privacy settings. Disabling cookies may impact features such as saved preferences or login-free personalization.

Contact

If you have questions about our use of cookies, reach out via the Simplify My Life contact form.

Privacy Policy

We respect your privacy and are committed to being transparent about how Simplify My Life collects, uses, and safeguards your information across our family of applications.

Last updated: June 10, 2024

Information we collect

Simplify My Life does not require account creation. We only collect the information you voluntarily share—such as your name, email address, and message—when you submit the contact form. Some of our hosted apps may gather anonymous analytics or diagnostic data to help us improve performance.

How we use your information

The information you share is used solely to respond to your inquiry, deliver requested updates, or troubleshoot issues. We do not sell or rent personal information and we only retain it for as long as necessary to provide support and maintain our services.

Cookies and third-party services

Some Simplify My Life products rely on cookies or local storage to remember your preferences. Embedded third-party widgets—such as Buy Me a Coffee or analytics tools—may set their own cookies according to their policies. Refer to the Cookie Policy for more details and instructions on updating your preferences.

Your choices and rights

  • Request access to the personal information you have shared.
  • Ask us to update or delete your contact details.
  • Opt out of optional communications or analytics at any time.

To exercise these rights, email us through the contact form and we will respond within a reasonable timeframe.

Contact

Questions about this policy or our data practices can be submitted via the Simplify My Life contact form.

Accessibility Statement

Simplify My Life is committed to creating inclusive experiences. This statement summarizes our approach to accessibility and the steps we take to support all visitors.

Last updated: June 10, 2024

Our commitment

We aim to meet or exceed WCAG 2.1 AA guidelines where practical. Each new feature is reviewed for keyboard navigation, color contrast, and assistive technology compatibility.

Ongoing improvements

Accessibility is a continuous process. We regularly test new releases, document known issues, and address feedback to improve usability for everyone.

Known limitations

Some prototype or experimental apps may have limited accessibility support. We clearly label early releases and provide alternative ways to accomplish critical actions when possible.

Feedback

If you encounter accessibility barriers, please let us know through the Simplify My Life contact form so we can address them promptly.
